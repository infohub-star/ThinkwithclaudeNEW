Programme
Three sessions, one hour each, on your business
Session 1
Foundations: Claude setup, prompting, and content
Configure Claude for your business and produce a week of content using your brand voice.
  • Set up Claude Projects with your brand voice, offers, and target customer
  • Apply the RACT prompt framework for consistent, on-brand outputs
  • Draft a week of LinkedIn posts, emails, and blog content on your business
  • Build a reusable prompt library for recurring content tasks
Duration: 1 Hour
Session 2
Research, proposals, and client work
Apply Claude to active leads, proposals, and client deliverables.
  • Run structured market and competitor research on your industry
  • Convert discovery notes into a complete client proposal
  • Build a lead nurture and DM response system
  • Organise client work in dedicated Claude Projects for consistency
Duration: 1 Hour
Session 3
Systems, team workflows, and deployment
Set up workflows and templates that make Claude a permanent part of your operations.
  • Build a master Project containing SOPs, brand assets, and pricing
  • Use Claude Skills for analysis, long-form writing, and research
  • Standardise hiring, onboarding, and HR documents in Claude
  • Create a 30-day plan to deploy Claude across your top three workflows
Duration: 1 Hour
